Who's Ready for a BOYZ NIGHT!

"DYK" is a group created by popular Twitch streamers, BruceDropEmOff, YourRageGamimg, and Kai Cenat. Each letter in the group represents each streamers' supportive community: DEO, YRG, and Kai Mafia.

"BruceDropEmOff" a.k.a Bruce Ray Condones

Bruce was born on September 23, 2000 and is from Riverdale, Georgia. He started streaming in 2016 through livestreaming games such as Roblox, Fornite, and Minecraft. Now, Bruce holds 24,716 active subscribers to his channel, placing him at #16 on the leaderboard of current active subscribers.

"YourRageGamimg" a.k.a Joshua

Joshua was born on August 17, 1997 and is from South Florida. He started streaming in 2019, all while gaining his fame while not having a facecam. Currently, Joshua holds 21,469 active subscribers to his channel, placing him at #20 on the leaderboard of current active subscribers.

Kai Cenat

Kai Cenat was born on December 16, 2001 and is from the Bronx, New York. He started streaming in February of 2021, which is outstanding considering the accomplishments he's made in the Twitch community since then. Recently, Kai has just made history as the most subscribed to streamer during black history month with 300,000 subscribers.
